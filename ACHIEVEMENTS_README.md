# Achievements Tracker

Track your performance review achievements with a Linear-inspired interface.

## Features

- **Achievement Categories**:
  - Feature Development
  - Customer Support
  - Cross-Team Cooperation
  - Other Contributions

- **Fields for Each Achievement**:
  - Category: Type/area of work
  - Description: What you did
  - Impact: Results, outcomes, or notes

- **Auto-populated from Your Work**:
  - 12 major feature implementations
  - 151+ customer support tickets
  - 130+ cooperation tickets
  - Linked Jira ticket keys

## Usage

1. **Navigate to Achievements**
   - Click "Achievements" in the top navigation
   - Or visit `/achievements`

2. **Fill Out Achievement Details**
   - Category: e.g., "Feature Development", "Customer Support"
   - Description: Brief explanation of what you accomplished
   - Impact: Quantifiable results or business impact

3. **Save Your Progress**
   - Click "Save" button or press `Cmd+S`
   - Data persists locally in `achievements_data.json`

4. **View Statistics**
   - Top stats cards show totals by category
   - Feature Work
   - Support Tickets
   - Cooperation count
